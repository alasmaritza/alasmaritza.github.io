12 for 12 Campaign

In reading the Bible, we find the number 12 numerous times in both the old and new testaments. The book of Genesis reveals that there were 12 sons of Jacob that made up the 12 tribes of Israel. Jesus had 12 apostles. In the book of Revelation the kingdom of God has 12 gates guarded by 12 angels.

In following the Bible's direction, we are excited to kick off a 12 for 12 fund raising campaign. We are looking for individuals to partner with SMP and commit to donating $12 per month for the 12 months of the year. This money will go to fund Salary Support and will ensure that the mission will continue to share the love of Jesus Christ with the people of El Salvador. To get signed up for the 12 for 12 campaign just click on the subscribe PayPal link below to get started. Setting up payments with PayPal is free, secure and easy. Once you are set up, payments will be withdrawn automatically so won't have to worry about forgetting.
